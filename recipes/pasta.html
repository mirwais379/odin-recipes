Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a traditional Italian food consisting of cooked pasta. Usually this is served with cheese and a tomato base and garnished with basil.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoe base
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Add the tomato base ontop of the pasta
  3. Put cheese ontop of the tomato base
  4. Put the basil leaves on top of the pasta and garnish
