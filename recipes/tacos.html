Taco Recipe

Description

A Taco is a traditional Mexican food consisting of a small hand-sized corn- or wheat-based tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Tortilla
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Chop the tomatoes
  3. Put chopped beef and tomatoes into the tortilla
  4. Squeeze the lemon onto the tortilla and wrap
